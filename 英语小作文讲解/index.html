看似类别很多，其实都是应用型文本，而且没有偏离日常生活太多。核心还在于清晰的思路和适配情景的内容。合适的句式是辅助表达的。

从24年开始，对情景的要求似乎有所提升。

结构分析#

小作文就写三段

  • 开头，寒暄，点名写信目的
    • 称呼语
    • 目的
    • 可以适当引入情境，比如说明背景、说明自己的身份
  • 中间，主体部分，完成任务
    • 要充实，不套作
    • 一般写两点就够了
  • 结尾，按照体裁和情境回应任务，完成写作
    • 注意落款和称呼

开头的各种客套的话可以积累

  • more than delighted to
  • quite pleased to
  • very grateful to
  • glad to hear from you
  • on behalf of…
  • I’m deeply sorry to hear that

中间通常要结构清晰

  • First, Second
  • For one thing, For another
  • On the one hand, on the other hand

结尾有一些小心思

  • Attached to this email is detailed information you may find helpful

常用的落款

结尾客套语适用情形与关系
Yours sincerely最常用、最安全的选择。 适用于大多数知道收信人姓名的情况（如写给教授、经理、机构负责人）。语气礼貌、正式且通用。
Yours faithfully,非常正式。 传统上用于你不知道收信人具体姓名的信件开头（如用 Dear Sir or Madam）。在现代用法中略显古板，但使用绝对正确。
Yours truly,比"Sincerely"稍显随意一点，但仍属正式范畴。可用于与有一定熟悉度的专业人士通信。
Best regards, / Kind regards,现代、礼貌、专业。 在商务和正式通信中越来越流行。比"Yours sincerely"略显亲切，但又不失礼貌，而且表达重视。非常推荐使用。
Yours,通用、稍显随意。 可用于熟悉的朋友或同事。在考研中，如果题目要求是写给朋友，这是一个不错的选择。
Best wishes,友好、亲切。 非常适合写给朋友、熟人，表达良好的祝愿。在给编辑或熟悉团体的建议信中也可使用。

Sincerely 系列的致敬词属于通用型的结尾方式，可正式、可随意，不挑场合； Regards 系列更为正式一些，在重视、尊重对方的情况下，比较适合使用Best regards; Kind regards 这样的致敬词， Truly 系列（ Yours truly; Truly yours）则稍显亲密，更适合朋友之间的通信

按照体裁#

建议#

开头

  • 直接说明自己是要建议，迅速进入正题
  • 如果有情景，可以客套一下

I am writing to give some suggestions on I’m writing to suggest ways to I’m so glad to give some suggestions I’m wrting for the purpose of

结尾

  • 祝福、期待
  • 继续联系

There is no universal recipe for good translation, but I am sure the advice above can help you on XXXX

l am convinced that you will enjoy a comfortableand fruitful trip of Chinese culture.

I bet you will….

If you need further clarifications, do not hesitate to contact me at any time

邀请#

开头

  • 说明因为什么邀请，事件是什么
  • 表达邀请

An international innovation contest will be held at our university from September 21st-25th and we are very glad to invite you to organize a team for the event.

l am writing to extend a cordial invitation to you for our graduation ceremony, scheduled to take place at the Students’ Center on December 31,2024, from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.

I’m writing to request your attendance to a dinner party that is to be held in Beijingin August.

An English speech contest is scheduled to start at 19:00 on June 20 in the Center of Students’Activities and….

结尾

  • 重申欢迎和客套
  • 说明截止时间

Please confirm your availability on or before August 1st by replying to this email. We are looking forward to your participation.

Your consideration of this invitation will be highly appreciated!

推荐#

开头一般有场景

It’s my absolute pleasure to recommend

I’m more than delighted to recommend XX to you

As for … , here is a bit of my personnal experience

通知#

开头简明扼要：This notice is to inform that 时间、地点、事件、额外需求

结尾强调通知的目的和联系方式

hose interested in joining it may send an email to s-university@edu.cn before July 1st, for application and interview screening prior to volunteering in the summer camp

感谢#

I am writing to extend my heartfelt thanks/sincere gratitude toward

I’m so glad to receive your invitation

Thanks a lot for your kind gesture of congratulating on my success

欢迎#

I am writing to extend my sincere greetings/warmest welcome to xxx

投诉#

I am writing to complain about the poor quality of a product that l purchased from yourstore two weeks ago

道歉#

开篇直接道歉

l sincerely apologize for (my decision to / failure to)

结尾再次道歉，可以提出补偿：

Please accept my apologies for any inconvenience it might have caused, and I will surely help in any way with the transition process in the coming weeks

申请#

开头可以说明身份，增强代入感

I’m writing for an opportunity to work in your esteemed company.

As a senior student from Peking University who isquite interested in cultural communication, I am writing for the purpose of applying for the volunteer position at your center.

结尾保证自己会努力

If I am lucky enough to be a volunteer for your center,l will do my utmost to fulfill my duties.

会议纪要（新题型）#

通常情况下，一篇纪要需要包含会议的基本信息，如时间、地点、参会人员、会议主题、会议内容等，会议内容可能涉及会议各环节做了什么、最终得出什么结论等。

没考过，我看Fiona给出的方式如下：

$\textrm{I}$. Meeting Details Location:Conference room at the Headquarters Date: October 10,2022 Time: 15:00-16:00 Attendees: Johnson Smith,Tom Jones, Betty Brown, and Andrew Taylor

$\textrm{II}$. Meeting Objective Develop our marketing strategy for the next year.

$\textrm{III}$. Meeting Agenda

  1. Johnson reviewed last year’s performance of the marketing team, and he explained how important it is to elevate collaborative competence.
  2. Betty came out with a proposal for team building, and her suggestions were listed down in the PowerPoint for everyone’s reference.
  3. lt was decided to improve the marketing performance in a team-based environment, and the business plan for 2023 had been finalized at the end of the meeting.
