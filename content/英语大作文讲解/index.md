无论是漫画还是图表，还是材料作文，都是三段式结构。

个人以为，**万物转社会现象**

| 段落  | 核心任务      | 万能句式模板（括号内为示例）                         |
| --- | --------- | -------------------------------------- |
| 首段  | 描述图片，点题   | 图中具体展示了什么行为？主题是什么？                     |
| 第二段 | 扩大现象、分析原因 | 图片的现象及其主题，**关联了什么更深刻的社会现象**？为什么会产生这种现象 |
| 第三段 | 呼吁行动      | 回归主题，提出建议                              |

### 第一段

总起句+描述句+（过渡句）。无论是描述画面还是描述数据，核心是清晰的逻辑！

#### 总起句

##### 漫画作文

如果是漫画作文，点主题：
- The picture **offers a glimpse into/of...
- The picture allows us to closely examine / tap into...
- The picture **sharpens the focus on**...
- **The pair of pictures** gives us a window to see
- The cartoonist, through this vivid depiction, **aims to highlight**...

一些更具体的写法，但是或许没必要
- This drawing offers **a poignant caricature of**...（深刻的讽刺）
- This **satirical drawing** offers a insightful glimpse into（用形容词形容漫画，概括情感）
- The picture depics an extreme cases of...

一定要**提炼出主题**


> glimpse into 和 glimpse of 的区别：接 into 时可引出较为抽象的“洞察”或“见解”；接of时是具体场景、事物

例句：

1. The picture offers an inspiring glimpse into the choice of “quitting" versus “persevering"
2. The picture allows us to tap into the deeper significance of mutual support in a family
3. The picture allows us to tap into the importance of cultivating a good habit
4. The picture invites our attention to the communication problems in today's digital age.

##### 图表作文

如果是图表作文，说明对象：
- The bar/pie/line chart **illustrates** XXX among/in a certain group (时间限定)
- The XX chart **invites our attention** to...
- The XX chart **offers a holistic view of**...
- The bar chart effectively **provides a clear comparison**（对比图）

柱子是**垂直方向**时柱状图：column chart；横着的柱状图：bar；饼状图：pie；折线图：line graph；表格：table

> 我建议不要点深层主题，而是说明描述的对象，因为图表内容丰富，描述起来复杂


例句：

1. The pie chart effectively **illustrates** the various ways students use their mobile phones in a particular university.
2. The pie chart allows us to **closely examine** why *college students* travel.
3. The pie chart offers an informative **glimpse into** the purposes for reading on a smartphone *among college students in a certain university*
4. The line chart **showcases** the changes in health literacy levels *among residents from 2012 to 2021.*
5. The bar chart effectively **provides a clear comparison** of the volume ofdelivery service  *between rural areas and the whole country* from 2018 to 2020.



#### 漫画描述句

衔接总起句，然后开始描述画面内容

- 因为水平有限，先用万能动词：go, do, move, get, take, hold；常见动作 sit, stand, say
- 用一般现在时或  现在进行时

思路：
1. 找到中心事物，首先用**there be句型**或者直接**主谓宾**描述中心；
2. 如果还要继续描述别的，用**方位词+倒装**点出其位置，然后开始新一轮描述。灵活使用**非谓语 with**，实现句式的多变。
3. **背景 + 核心人物/事物 + 动作/状态 + 引语**

例句：
1. In the left drawing, **there is** a young guy, **reading** a paper book attentively in the library. / **Across from** the table **sits** a girl, focusing on a tablet computer.
2. As can be seen, **there is** a father，**with** a smartphone in his hands, **sitting** leisurely on a sofa **and staring** at the screen. / **Beside him sits** his son, **with** perplexity on his face, asking,"Dad, areyou listening to me?" 
3. As can be seen, **there is** a dragon boat competition, **with** two teams paddling strenously on dragon boats. / **On the bridge above**, a crowd of spectators gathers, **among whom** one old couple, holding each other, and says "Great! The dragon boat racing of our vilidge is getting more popularity".
4. It can be seen that, a father **stands** in front of his son, **encouraging** him to get rid of concern about studying Chinese drama if it is what he truly likes

#### 图表描述句

##### 静态图

柱状图、饼状图这种表示占比的，就是静态图。找出**最大值、最小值、有特点的值**，用表示 **“占比”** 的句型进行描述。

占比句型：
- **accounts for** 84.8%, **comprise** 91.3%, **represent** 23.8%，**stands at** 32.6%，make up 30%
- 可以适当加形容词：a massive 75% / an overwhelming 80%
- **29% of** people exercise with friends,family
- constitute / make up / account for 后面只能接百分数，constitute 后面没有形容词，只接数据的话，这个百分数前面千万不要加 a，stand at 后面既可以接数字也可以接百分数


思路：**降序排列**，使用恰当的**逻辑连词**和表达
1. 先声夺人描述极大值：
	- 连词：Notably, xxxx / The most notable observation is
	- 描述：exceed all other categories / have the largest proportion / accounts for the largest proportion
2. 然后按照占比递减顺序描述：
	- 连词：Following this, xxxx / Next is xxxx / while / （完全衔接上句）This is followed by B, which represents 30%
3. 最低值：
	- 连词：Finally comes 
	- 描述：Only a mere 10% prefer to... / Only a small minority
4. 其他技巧：
	1. 多个数据：**Meanwhile**,​ the figures for C and D are 20% and 10% **respectively**.
	2. **描述“相似”**：similarly, equally, both...and...
	3. 引出重点：A particularly notable point is that... / It is worth noting that... / What stands out is that...


例句：
1. （展现一下复杂思路）This is a column chart, clearly illustrating the significant contrasts in the proportions of AI productusers in different age groups in 2024. **To be specific**, those aged from 20 to 29 **have the largest proportion** of AIusers, **accounting for** 40.5%. **The next are** those aged under 20, at 29.1%, **followed by** those aged from 30 to 39 and those in the 40-49 group, **at** 23.7% and 17.1% respectively. **Finally come** those aged from 50 to 59 andthose over 59, **at** 10.4% and 5.4% respectively.

2. The pie chart illustrates how people access news. Notably, online platforms are the most popular source, **accounting for a significant**​ 58%. **This is followed by**​ television, which **represents**​ 28% of the responses. Meanwhile, the figures for radio and print newspapers are much lower, **at**​ 10% and a mere 4% **respectively**.

3. As can be seen, the percentage stands at a massive 54.30% for people who prefer exercising alone, followed by 47.70% of those exercising with friends. By contrast, only a small minority prefer exercising with family (23.90%) or in groups (15.80%).


##### 动态图

折线图，几个指标的按年份排列的柱状图，都表达了一种趋势。描述**总体趋势**（上升、下降、稳定）和**关键时间段**的变化

思路：如果整体有趋势，就先说趋势，再描述；如果没有，就各自描述变化情况。注意连词。不要描述每一个数据点，描述最高最低值、关键转折点、数据交汇点

趋势和变化句型
- 不变：remain steady at roughly 25%
- 波动：fluctuated widely/wildly/sharply/moderately/significantly；fluctuated between A and B
- 增加/减少：
	- 总体描述：**an upward trend is visible** / we can witness an upward trend / have seen an rapid increase / experienced significant rise
	- 细节描述：rise from 10% in January to 25% in June / stood at 10% in January and jumped to 25% at June / soaring from 637.8 million to 781.1 million
	- 词汇：
		- 快速：drop/fall/plunge   surge/soar/rocket
		- 适度：drop/decline/dip(小幅度会回升)   increase/rise/grow/climb   **Growth**
- 登顶：peak at / reach a peak of
- 触底：bottom out at / reach a low of
- 关系：surpass, overtake, converge
- 形容词： slightly，sharp，dramatic，moderate，considerable
- 精确时间：between 2010 and 2020, over the 10-year period, from January to June
- 连词：Conversely，whereas，in contrast

| 功能/场景   | 核心词汇与句型                                                                                      |
| ------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 描述趋势    | **动词**: increase/rise/grow, decrease/fall/drop, fluctuate, stabilize                         |
|         | **名词**: an upward trend, a downward trend, a period of stability, fluctuations               |
| 修饰变化程度  | **急剧**: sharp, dramatic, rapid, significant, skyrocket, plunge                               |
|         | **适度**: moderate, steady, considerable, noticeable                                           |
|         | **轻微**: slight, marginal, gradual, edge up, creep up                                         |
| 描述波动    | fluctuate widely/sharply/slightly, fluctuate between A and B                                 |
| 描述极值点   | **顶点**: peak at, reach a peak/high of, the highest point                                     |
|         | **底点**: bottom out at, reach a low of, the lowest point                                      |
| 引入数据与时间 | from...to..., between...and..., over the period, stood at, starting at, ending at, by (+时间点) |
| 进行逻辑对比  | **对比**: In contrast, By way of contrast, Conversely, Whereas, While                          |
|         | **引出细节**: Notably, Specifically, In particular, A closer look reveals that...                |


例句
1. To be specific, **an upward trend is visible** in the nationwide volume of parcel deliveries. In 2018, the number of parcels **stood at** 51 billion, which **jumped to** 83 billion in 2020. **When narrowing the focus to** rural areas, there was also a strong growth, with the volume of parcels increasing from 12 billion to 30 billion during the same 3-year period

2. **Notably**, the proportion of individuals with health literacy **remained stable from 2012 to 2016**. Initially at approximately 10%, it then **experienced a significant rise, increasing from** 14.18% in 2017 to 25.4% in 2021.

3. This is a table, clearly illustrating the changes in thedomestic sales of gasoline-powered and new-energy vehiclesin China during the first half of 2025. In this period, the sales of new-energy cars increased steadily, **from 0.79 million in January to 1.12 million in June**. **By contrast**, the sales of gasoline cars **fluctuated around** 1.1 million. **It is particularly noteworthy that** the sales of new-energy vehicles surpassed those of gasoline-powered vehicles in May.


### 第二段

立论：指出社会现象——漫画的主题关联到什么现象、数据揭露的现象。**数据本身就是现象的体现**

分析原因：两到三点，句式丰富些

#### 立论

**衔接**对图画/数据的描述，并**转入**社会现象，建议使用类似下面的句式：

**What the data reveals** is not merely a change/a significant expansion in / noticeable preference for ... **but a deeper transformation** in...（数据揭示的不仅是...的改变/扩张/偏好，更是……的深层变革。）

个人认为这样的句式非常凝练，而且貌似没有哪个老师是这样教的，不会撞


#### 析因

结构化！建议写三个点

##### 让步法

如果前面的现象是负面现象，可以
- 让步：Admittedly, 
- 转折：However
- 递进：Even worse / What's more
- 归纳：

或者
- 让步
- 转折
- 升华

##### 直接法

总起句
- **The reasons behind this are multifaceted/twofold/threefold.**
- **This shift can be attributed to a confluence of factors**

结构
- 最经典的：First, Second, Third
- 逻辑：Primarily, Moreover, Besides
- 混搭：To begin with, In addition, Finally
- 更丰富的： This phenomenon can be primarily attributed to​ A.     Coupled with this is（在两个联系很紧密时使用）

| 功能     | 高分句式                                                                               | 说明                           |
| ------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| 引出原因   | A number of interlocking factors are at play here.                                 | 比 There are many reasons 更高级 |
| 分析根本原因 | **At the heart of this issue lies**…                                               | 强调核心原因                       |
|        | This is a symptom of a deeper problem: …                                           | 将现象视为“症状”，分析“病因”             |
| 从个人角度  | On an individual level, people are driven by…                                      | 分析个人心理动机                     |
| 从社会角度  | Culturally, we are witnessing a shift in…                                          | 分析社会文化变迁                     |
| 从技术角度  | Technologically, the proliferation of … has…                                       | 分析技术影响                       |
| 强调结果   | Consequently, this creates a cycle where A leads to B, which in turn reinforces A. | 分析恶性/良性循环，展现深度               |

最后会给出可以直接参考的模板，这里是提供多种句式供了解
